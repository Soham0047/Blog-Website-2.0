About Me

This is what I do.

Hi there! I’m Soham Bhowmick, a passionate Computer Science student currently pursuing my Bachelor’s degree at San Jose State University. I have a deep love for technology, problem-solving, and innovation, and I thrive on turning complex ideas into functional, user-friendly solutions.

As an international student from India, I’ve always been curious about how technology shapes our world. My journey started with exploring programming languages like C++, Python, and Java in high school, where I built my first small-scale apps. This curiosity turned into a full-fledged passion as I advanced to work on web development, machine learning, and software engineering projects during my academic career. From building a social media app using advanced data structures and algorithms to creating a machine-learning model that translates hand gestures into text, I have always loved applying technical knowledge to solve real-world challenges. Recently, I’ve explored tools like React.js, ModelSim, and machine learning libraries such as TensorFlow and scikit-learn, solidifying my expertise in both front-end and back-end development.

I created this blog to document my learning journey, share solutions, and connect with other tech enthusiasts like you. Whether you’re a fellow student, developer, or someone curious about technology, I hope this blog provides value through detailed tutorials, project breakdowns, and personal insights.
